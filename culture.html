Who We Are

Our Culture & Values

Built on a foundation of educational excellence, cultural respect, and technological innovation — Fanmo is more than a company; it is a movement to redefine how the world learns and grows.

Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations worldwide through exceptional educational services, cutting-edge technology, and authentic cultural experiences — delivering measurable growth, sustainable impact, and lasting value to every client we serve, from Nanjing to six continents.

We believe that quality education, when delivered with creativity, care, and cultural sensitivity, can transform lives, unlock human potential, build bridges across communities, and foster the global citizens our world needs. Education is our passion; innovation is our method; impact is our measure.

Through our thirteen integrated service categories — from curriculum consulting to mobile platforms, from art enrichment to corporate development — we address the complete educational ecosystem. We partner with schools, universities, corporations, and individuals to create learning experiences that matter: measurable academic growth, professional advancement, cultural fluency, and personal transformation.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted, innovative, and impactful education and cultural services company serving international markets — recognized for our integrity, our technology excellence, our cultural respect, and our unwavering commitment to client success.

We envision a future where Fanmo's integrated ecosystem of educational services, mobile platforms, and cultural programs is central to how millions of people discover their passions, develop marketable skills, build professional networks, and connect authentically with diverse cultures. By 2030, Fanmo will serve 500,000+ active users annually, operate with 100+ partner institutions across North America, Europe, Australia, and Asia, and maintain the highest standards of user privacy, data security, and educational ethics.

Our broader vision extends beyond commercial growth: we aspire to be a bridge builder connecting talented but underserved students in emerging markets with world-class educational resources, enabling cross-cultural understanding at scale, and demonstrating to organizations worldwide that thoughtfully designed, technology-enabled education delivers superior outcomes for individuals and societies alike.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision, every product, and every interaction at Fanmo Education Technology.

People First

Every program, product, and service begins with a deep understanding of the individual — their goals, their learning style, and their potential. Our people-first philosophy ensures genuine outcomes, not just transactions.

Excellence in Everything

From the quality of our mobile applications to the caliber of our instructors, we maintain uncompromising standards. Excellence is not a goal — it is a habit embedded in our organizational DNA.

Continuous Innovation

The education landscape is constantly evolving — and so are we. We invest heavily in R&D, technology, and new service models to ensure our clients always have access to the most effective educational solutions.

Cultural Respect

We celebrate diversity and believe cultural exchange is one of education's most powerful tools. Every program we design honors the traditions, perspectives, and values of the communities we serve globally.

Integrity & Transparency

We are honest in all our dealings — with clients, partners, regulators, and our own team. From our data privacy practices to our business contracts, transparency is non-negotiable.

Community Impact

We measure our success not just by revenue, but by the positive impact we create in communities. From subsidized art programs to free study tour resources, Fanmo gives back wherever we operate.

Our Journey

The Fanmo Story

2015

Company Founded in Nanjing

Nanjing Fanmo Education Technology Co., Ltd was established in Jianye District, Nanjing, by a team of educators and entrepreneurs with a shared vision of transforming education through technology and cultural engagement. Initial focus: educational consulting services for K-12 schools and training centers across Jiangsu Province.

2016

Art Training Programs Launched

Fanmo launched structured non-academic art training programs, including painting (oil, watercolor), calligraphy (multiple traditional scripts), drawing, and basic sculpture. First cohort: 200+ students across Nanjing. Established relationships with 5 partner schools.

2017

Corporate Team-Building & Conference Services Launch

Fanmo expanded into the corporate market by launching custom team-building programs and conference management services. First enterprise clients: technology companies and manufacturing firms in Jiangsu. Managed first regional education conference with 300+ attendees in Nanjing.

2018

International Study Tours Begin

Fanmo expanded its study tour operations, planning and executing first international educational travel programs. Destinations: Japan (Tokyo, Kyoto), Singapore, and South Korea. Served 8 partner schools with total 500+ student participants. Established international partnerships with local educational coordinators.

2019

Marketing & Design Services; Graphic Studio Expansion

Fanmo added educational marketing consulting and founded an in-house creative studio. Services: brand strategy, digital marketing campaigns, UI/UX design, educational media production. First major project: complete rebrand and website overhaul for regional university partner.

2020

Digital Transformation & First Mobile App Launch

Fanmo launched its first mobile management application in response to COVID-19, enabling remote class scheduling, video tutoring coordination, and service booking. App downloaded 50,000+ times in first year across iOS and Android. Marked formal transition to EdTech company with hybrid online-offline service model.

2021

Educational Supplies Business & Partnerships Expansion

Fanmo added curated retail offerings of art supplies, office materials, books, and educational equipment. Simultaneously expanded institutional partnerships to 20+ schools and corporate training firms. Began serving clients in Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Suzhou regions.

2022

International Market Expansion — North America & Europe Entry

Fanmo began actively serving international clients across North America and Europe. Established partnerships with 8 universities and 12 corporate clients in USA and Germany. Launched multilingual (English/Chinese) version of mobile platform. Introduced GDPR-compliant data practices for EU operations.

2023

10,000+ Annual Users Milestone & Regulatory Compliance

Fanmo surpassed 10,000 annual active users across 15+ countries. Achieved full CCPA, GDPR, and UK GDPR compliance. Expanded study tour operations to 4 continents (Asia, Europe, North America, Oceania). Signed partnerships with 35+ educational institutions globally. Mobile app reached 300,000+ total downloads.

2024

Multi-Platform App Suite & Global Institutional Network

Fanmo released its comprehensive multi-platform mobile application suite on Google Play and the Apple App Store, integrating all 13 service categories. Achieved 40+ formal institutional partnerships across Asia-Pacific, North America, and Europe. App featured enterprise dashboard for corporate clients. Launched supplementary in-app learning content.

2025+

Continued Global Expansion & Strategic Growth

Fanmo operates with 50+ institutional partners across six continents, serving 15,000+ annual users across 25+ countries. Currently expanding into South American markets and deepening partnerships with Fortune 500 companies' corporate learning divisions. On track to reach 500,000 annual users by 2030 through strategic partnerships and market expansion.
