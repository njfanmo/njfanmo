About Us

A Technology-First
Education Partner

Nanjing Fanmo Education Technology Co., Ltd is headquartered in Jianye District, Nanjing, China. Established in 2015, we are dedicated to bridging world-class educational resources with learners and organizations across global markets. Our mission is to empower individuals and institutions through innovative education technology, cultural enrichment, and comprehensive learning solutions.

Our expertise spans thirteen core service categories: educational consulting and strategic planning; cutting-edge EdTech software and hardware research and development; international cultural and artistic exchange programs; literary creation and publication support; non-academic art training across multiple disciplines; traditional calligraphy and fine-arts interest guidance; comprehensive study tour planning and execution; corporate team-building and leadership development; educational supplies and cultural materials retail; strategic marketing and communications planning; conference and event management; professional graphic design and production services; and mobile application platforms available on Google Play and the Apple App Store.

With over 50 partner institutions across Asia, Europe, and North America, and serving more than 10,000 users annually, Fanmo brings a proven track record of delivering measurable educational outcomes. Our multidisciplinary team includes experienced educators, software engineers, experience designers, project managers, and cultural specialists — all united by a commitment to innovation and excellence.

Whether you are a school seeking curriculum innovation, a corporation planning enrichment activities, an individual pursuing artistic development, or a government institution enhancing educational capacity — Fanmo delivers comprehensive, end-to-end solutions designed for sustainable, measurable growth.
