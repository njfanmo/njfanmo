FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We reply to most inquiries within 2 business days. Enterprise and partnership requests are prioritized and receive structured follow-up from our specialist team.

Can I visit your office in person?

Yes, but all visits are by appointment only. Please contact us in advance to schedule a meeting, workshop, or project review. International visitors are welcome and English support is available.

What types of projects do you support?

We support educational consulting, EdTech R&D, cultural and artistic exchange, art training, study tours, enterprise team building, mobile app development, and more. See our Services page for details.

How do I propose a partnership?

For partnership proposals, please email ganlin@njfanmo.com with your organization profile, objectives, and collaboration outline. We will arrange a discovery call if there is a fit.

Is remote collaboration possible?

Absolutely. We work with clients worldwide via video meetings, digital platforms, and collaborative tools. Secure document exchange and project management are standard for all engagements.
