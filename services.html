What We Offer

Our Services

Thirteen integrated service verticals — from educational consulting to mobile technology — serving institutions, enterprises, and individuals worldwide.

Educational Consulting Services

We provide comprehensive strategic advisory for educational institutions, corporations, and individuals. Our consulting services cover curriculum architecture, academic program evaluation, international accreditation guidance, learner outcome analysis, and institutional development planning. We work with K-12 schools, universities, tutoring centers, and corporate learning departments to build education strategies that are measurable, scalable, and globally relevant.

Curriculum Design Academic Planning Program Evaluation Institutional Development

Educational Software & Hardware R&D

Fanmo's technology team designs, develops, promotes, and maintains educational software and hardware products. Our portfolio includes learning management systems (LMS), interactive classroom hardware integrations, student assessment platforms, adaptive learning engines, and the Fanmo mobile management application suite published on Google Play and the Apple App Store. We follow industry best practices for security, accessibility (WCAG 2.1), and cross-platform compatibility.

Mobile App Development LMS Platforms Hardware Integration Adaptive Learning Google Play App Store

Cultural & Artistic Exchange Activities

We organize and facilitate international cultural exchange programs that bring together students, educators, and artists from different countries and backgrounds. Activities include cross-border art exhibitions, joint educational seminars, cultural immersion workshops, international visiting artist residencies, and collaborative performances. These programs are designed to foster global citizenship, mutual understanding, and creative collaboration.

Art Exhibitions Cross-Border Programs Cultural Immersion International Exchange

Literary Creation

Fanmo supports and facilitates literary creation programs for learners and professionals. This includes creative writing workshops, storytelling for educational purposes, bilingual content development, educational fiction and non-fiction publication support, and literary competitions. Our literary creation services connect authors with educators and institutions to produce meaningful, educational, and culturally resonant written works.

Creative Writing Storytelling Workshops Content Development Literary Competitions

Art Training (Non-Academic)

Our non-academic art training programs offer structured yet accessible pathways for learners of all ages who want to develop artistic skills outside the formal curriculum. Disciplines include oil painting, watercolor, sketching, digital illustration, sculpture, ceramics, and mixed media. Courses are available in group workshop settings, private instruction formats, and via our mobile application for self-paced learning. All programs are taught by credentialed, experienced instructors.

Painting Drawing Digital Art Sculpture Workshop Private Instruction

Calligraphy & Fine Arts Interest Guidance

We offer specialized interest guidance programs in traditional Chinese calligraphy (running script, regular script, seal script, cursive script) and fine arts. These programs are designed for both Chinese heritage learners and international enthusiasts who wish to appreciate and practice this distinctive art form. Guided instruction covers brush technique, ink preparation, composition, historical context, and appreciation of classical works. Fine arts guidance spans both Eastern and Western traditions.

Chinese Calligraphy Brush Technique Fine Arts Heritage Arts Beginner to Advanced

Study Tour Activity Planning

Fanmo designs and manages comprehensive educational travel experiences for schools, universities, and corporate groups. Our study tour services include destination research and selection, themed itinerary design (arts, science, history, culture), safety planning, travel logistics, accommodation coordination, on-tour educational facilitation, and post-tour reporting. We operate programs across key educational destinations in Asia, Europe, North America, and Australia, ensuring compliance with local regulatory requirements for group educational travel.

Itinerary Design Educational Travel Safety Planning Logistics Asia Europe North America

Corporate Team Building Planning

We develop and execute bespoke corporate team-building programs that integrate creative arts, cultural experiences, and collaborative challenges. Formats include one-day workshops, multi-day retreats, virtual team-building sessions, and hybrid programs. Activities may incorporate painting workshops, calligraphy sessions, cultural cooking classes, escape room challenges, leadership simulations, and more. All programs are customizable to company size, industry, and corporate culture objectives.

Corporate Events Leadership Programs Creative Workshops Retreats Virtual Programs

Educational & Office Supplies Sales

Fanmo supplies a curated range of office supplies, cultural and sports supplies, books, stationery, and educational materials. Our product catalog is designed to support the learning environments we help create — from classroom stationery kits to professional art supply bundles, sport and recreational equipment for school programs, and reference books across academic and artistic disciplines. Products are available to institutional clients, corporate buyers, and individual learners.

Stationery Art Supplies Books Office Supplies Sports Equipment

Market Marketing Planning

Our marketing planning services help educational institutions, training programs, and EdTech products reach their target audiences effectively. We develop comprehensive marketing strategies including brand positioning, digital marketing campaigns (SEO, SEM, social media), content marketing, influencer partnerships, email marketing, and market research. We specialize in educational sector marketing for both domestic Chinese markets and international expansion into North American and European markets.

Brand Strategy Digital Marketing SEO & SEM Content Marketing Market Research

Conference & Meeting Services

Fanmo provides full-service conference and meeting management for educational conferences, academic summits, training symposia, and corporate meetings. Services include venue sourcing and setup, delegate registration systems, agenda design and speaker management, audio-visual production, simultaneous interpretation coordination, catering liaison, networking event design, and post-event feedback management. We manage events from 20 to 2,000 attendees with equal professionalism.

Conference Management Speaker Management AV Production Delegate Registration Networking Events

Graphic Design & Media Production

Our creative studio delivers professional graphic design and media production services for educational organizations and businesses. The studio's capabilities include brand identity systems, printed educational materials, digital interface design (UI/UX), presentation design, infographics, educational animations, video production, photography direction, and social media asset creation. We combine aesthetic excellence with clear educational communication principles.

Brand Identity Print Design UI/UX Design Video Production Infographics
