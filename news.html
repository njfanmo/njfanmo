Fanmo Launches Next-Generation Mobile Education Platform Globally

Fanmo's new integrated mobile platform is now live on Google Play and the Apple App Store worldwide, offering a unified digital experience that unifies all 13 service categories. The platform features cross-platform synchronization, an adaptive learning engine, enterprise management dashboards for corporate clients, multilingual support (English/Chinese), secure payment integration, and automated invoicing for institutional users.

PartnershipApr 22, 2025

Strategic Partnerships Expand International Cultural Exchange Programs

Fanmo has signed formal partnership agreements with 8 new cultural organizations across the United States (Boston, Los Angeles), United Kingdom (London), and Germany (Berlin, Munich). These agreements establish cultural exchange pathways for 2025-2026, including artist residencies, student exchange programs, and collaborative educational exhibitions targeting 1,000+ participants.

AwardsMar 15, 2025

Fanmo Recognized for Educational Technology Innovation at National Summit

At the 2025 National Education Technology Summit in Beijing, Fanmo received the "Innovation Excellence Award" for its integrated service model combining EdTech, cultural enrichment, and business services. The award recognized measurable learner outcome improvements and the company's commitment to accessible, high-quality education technology for diverse global markets.

TechnologyFeb 28, 2025

Fanmo R&D Team Completes Adaptive Learning Engine for Mobile Platform

Fanmo's research and development team has completed a sophisticated adaptive learning engine that personalizes content delivery within the Fanmo mobile app. Using machine learning algorithms, the engine analyzes individual user progress, learning style preferences, session history, and engagement patterns to optimize educational content recommendations and pacing. Available to all app users starting March 2025.

Fanmo Hosts Annual International Calligraphy & Fine Arts Showcase in Nanjing

Fanmo's 2025 International Calligraphy & Fine Arts Showcase welcomed 350+ participants from 12 countries across Asia, Europe, and North America. The 3-day event featured live calligraphy demonstrations by master artists, curated fine arts exhibitions, cultural performances from traditional and contemporary disciplines, hands-on workshops, and networking sessions celebrating artistic tradition and innovation.
